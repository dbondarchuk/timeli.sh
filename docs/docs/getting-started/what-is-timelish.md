# What is Timelish?

**Timelish** (also written **Timeli.sh**) is an all-in-one tool for **booking appointments**. It helps you **take bookings online**, **stay on top of your calendar**, **message clients**, and **get paid** - and you can shape a simple **booking website** without writing code.

## Who is it for?

Timelish is built for **people and small businesses that sell their time**: consultants, coaches, clinics, salons, tutors, fitness studios, and similar businesses. It also suits anyone who wants a **clear, branded booking experience** on their own web address.

## What you can do with Timelish

- Let clients **book appointments online** anytime.
- Build **pages** that explain your services, answer common questions, and share contact details.
- **Send reminders** by email or text message.
- **Connect your calendars** - for example Google Calendar or Outlook - so your availability stays up to date.
- Offer **video visits** when you connect Zoom, Google Meet, or Microsoft Teams.
- **Take payments** when you link a service such as Stripe, Square, or PayPal (according to what is available in your region).

## The two sides of Timelish

| Where                        | What you use it for                                          |
| ---------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Your public booking site** | Clients see your pages and book time with you.               |
| **Your Timelish dashboard**  | You set up services, hours, branding, messages, and billing. |

The exact web address you use depends on your setup. When you join Timelish, your welcome steps or signup link will show where to sign in later.
