# Build your website

Your setup wizard already created starter pages for your booking site. Now open them in the visual editor and make them yours - no coding required.

Open **Website** → **Pages** in the side menu.

![Page builder](/img/placeholders/first-steps-page-builder.svg)

## Understand your page list

You will see a table of pages with names, URLs, and whether each page is **published**. Common starter pages include a home page, a services page, and pages for booking or contact.

Click a page row to open the **page builder**.

## Edit content with blocks

The editor shows your page as a stack of **blocks** - building pieces you can add, remove, and rearrange:

- **Text and headings** - introduce your business and explain what you offer.
- **Images** - photos of your work, team, or space.
- **Containers and columns** - group content side by side on wider screens.
- **Booking widget** - lets clients pick a service and time without leaving the page.

Click a block to edit it. Use the toolbar to add new blocks above or below existing ones. Changes are not live until you save and the page is published (covered in [Publish and share](./publish-and-share.md)).

## Upload images

Go to **Website** → **Assets** to upload photos and files. When you add an **Image** block on a page, pick from assets you have already uploaded.

## Headers and footers

Go to **Website** → **Appearance** to manage **page headers** and **page footers** - the top navigation bar and bottom section that repeat across pages.

- Create or edit a header/footer once, then assign it to each page in that page's settings panel.
- A clear header with your logo and a "Book now" link helps clients find what they need.

## Preview before going live

While editing a page, use the **preview** link to open the page in a new tab. Preview shows your work even before the page is published, so you can check layout on desktop and phone.

## Blocks from installed apps

If you turned on optional apps during setup, extra blocks may appear in the editor:

- **Blog** - news and updates
- **Forms** - contact or intake forms
- **Gift cards** - sell gift cards on your site

Install or enable these later from **Apps** → **App Store** if you skipped them. See **[Apps overview](/docs/apps/overview)**.

## A sensible first pass

You do not need a perfect site on day one. A practical first edit:

1. Update the home page headline and a short welcome paragraph.
2. Confirm your services page lists what you offer.
3. Add your logo to the header.
4. Replace any placeholder images with real photos.

You can always return to add pages, blog posts, or forms as your business grows.
